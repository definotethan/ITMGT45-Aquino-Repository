Dalgona
120 pesos
Dalgona is a whipped coffee drink made by blending instant coffee, sugar, and hot water into a fluffy, caramel-like foam served over milk. Light, creamy, and mildly sweet, it’s perfect for those who enjoy a fun twist on iced coffee with a smooth, frothy texture.
